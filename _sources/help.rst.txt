Acknowledgement
***************
The original work on deepQuest has been funded by the EAMT_.

The implementation of the deepQuest framework follows the architecture and style of the NMT-Keras_ library, developed by `Marc Bolaños`_ and `Álvaro Peris`_.

Related projects
****************

`Lucia Specia`_ is a pioneer in Quality Estimation, and with `her team`_, she has released numerous resources to support research work on this topic: 

* `Quest++`_: open source toolkit for pipelined multi-level translation Quality Estimation for statistical Machine Translation, based on feature extraction (written in Java)
* `Marmot`_: open source toolkit for word-level QE (written in Python)
* `Resources`_: a collection of datasets to support work on Quality Estimation


How to contribute?
******************
If you wish to support the development of DeepQuest, by contributing to its code, or by making your approach available through it, please get in touch by sending an email to `Lucia Specia`_.

*********************
